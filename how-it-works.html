Frequently Asked Questions

What is HORECA verification? expand_more

HORECA stands for Hotel, Restaurant, and Catering. We verify every business account to ensure all marketplace participants are legitimate professionals. This maintains food safety standards and professional decorum.

How does local pickup work? expand_more

Once a transaction is accepted, the seller shares their business address and preferred pickup windows. Buyers are responsible for arranging transport. We recommend verifying the items during the handover.

Is there a fee for using BackHouse? expand_more

Joining and browsing is free. We charge a small service fee on successful transactions to cover platform maintenance, payment security, and our verification team's efforts.

What types of items can I list? expand_more

BackHouse supports non-perishable surplus: dry goods, packaging, cleaning supplies, kitchen equipment, textiles, and similar hospitality inventory. Perishable items are not currently supported.
