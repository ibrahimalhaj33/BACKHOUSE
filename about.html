The BackHouse Story

Founded in 2022, BackHouse began as a response to the stark contrast between food surplus in high-end hospitality and the logistical challenges of redistributing resources. We recognized that the circular economy wasn't just an environmental necessity, but a business opportunity for Jordan’s hotels and restaurants.

Our founders, veterans of the logistics and hospitality industries, envisioned a digital concierge that could automate the discovery and exchange of surplus inventory. Today, we serve as the critical infrastructure for sustainable resource management in the region.
